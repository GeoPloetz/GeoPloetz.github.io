Research Profile

Geospatial geoarchaeologist studying ancient engineered landscapes, environmental legacies, and human–environment interaction in the Maya lowlands. My research integrates lidar, drone mapping, GIS, 3D modeling, excavation, coring, soils, paleoenvironmental reconstruction, and digital heritage to reconstruct how communities transformed tropical landscapes over time.
