CAVE Lab

Computational Archaeology and Visualized Environments

The CAVE Lab is my future research and teaching lab focused on ancient landscapes, geospatial science, environmental change, and digital heritage. The lab studies how people transform environments across time through integrated field, lab, and computational methods.

Through lidar, drone mapping, 3D scanning, geophysics, excavation, coring, soil and sediment analysis, remote sensing, VR, and 3D printing, the CAVE Lab will document ancient engineered landscapes and their modern environmental legacies.

Research

Ancient engineered landscapes, environmental legacies, settlement patterns, digital geospatial heritage, and long-term human–environment interaction.
